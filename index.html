How to use this site

For the best experience, start with your biggest travel need first and add extras after.

  1. Start with flights if you still need to book travel.
  2. Add a rental car or airport transfer based on your destination plans.
  3. Browse attractions, tickets, and city passes after your transportation is set.
  4. Use train and route tools if your trip includes multiple stops.
  5. Check compensation support if you had a disrupted flight.
  6. Add travel connectivity and other extras before departure.

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to help visitors understand how the site works.

Do I book directly on this site?

No. This site helps you compare travel options and then sends you to a trusted travel partner to complete your booking.

Does it cost extra to use these links?

No extra cost is added for using these partner links. Pricing and availability are handled by the booking partner.

Can I use only one category?

Yes. You can use just flights, just cars, just transfers, or any other single category that fits your trip.
