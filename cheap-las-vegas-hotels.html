The Economics of Sin City Stays: Bypassing Peak Surges

Finding an affordable room in Las Vegas requires an analytical look at how resort prices fluctuate throughout the standard weekly travel calendar.

The single most powerful variable in determining your room cost is your check-in day. Las Vegas acts as a major weekend playground for regional drive-in travelers and leisure tourists, which causes room rates to skyrocket on Friday and Saturday nights. Conversely, when the weekend crowd departs, massive four-star resorts frequently cut their room rates significantly to maintain steady occupancy lines before the mid-week business crowds roll in.
